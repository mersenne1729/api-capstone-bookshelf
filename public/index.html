Jeromes Bookshelf take inspiration from the Everyman's Library which was a series of reprinted classic literature in 1906. The idea It was conceived in 1905 by Joseph Malaby Dent, whose goal was to create a 1,000 volume library of world literature that was affordable for, and that appealed to, every kind of person, from students to the working classes to the cultural elite. Below is a modest collection of 54 book all concerning philosophy. The information provides a little about the book and author, while searching you can add either of these to your favourite to view later.
